About Me

My aim is always to make my designs & creativity come to life. Gunit's Boutique is an upcoming fashion Boutique for womens & children wear primarily deals in style assessment, designing, customized stitching, ready to wear, party wear, bridal wear & running fabric materials. Having more than six years of experience in designing dresses for our clients as per their needs & requirements & having satisfied them with perfect fit. I started this venture as my passion at a very small scale. Started gathering satisfied customer's appreciation & my clients multiplied through word of mouth. We dont only have customers limited to our area but also different parts of the world like Canada, Qatar, Malaysia, Singapore etc.
